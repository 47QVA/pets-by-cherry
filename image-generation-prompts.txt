PETS BY CHERRY — TEXT-TO-IMAGE PROMPTS
========================================
For use with Midjourney, DALL-E 3, Stable Diffusion, Adobe Firefly, etc.
These are prompts only — no images have been generated or added to the app.
Once you've picked final images, they can replace the files under public/images/.


SHARED STYLE GUIDE (apply to every prompt below)
-------------------------------------------------
Professional studio photography. Plain seamless background (light gray or soft
off-white, per the reference photo). Soft, even, diffused studio lighting with a
gentle catchlight — no harsh shadows, no dramatic/moody lighting. Sharp focus on
the subject with a shallow depth of field. Photorealistic — not illustration,
not 3D render, not cartoon. No people, no hands, no visible cages/tanks/props
unless the prompt says otherwise, no text, no watermarks, no logos. High
resolution.

Brand accent (optional, for category shots only): a warm pastel sage green
(#A9C98E) may tint the background or lighting instead of pure gray — keep it
subtle, not saturated.
